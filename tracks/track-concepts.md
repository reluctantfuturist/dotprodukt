### 02. Mr Shannon
**Working Title:** Mr Shannon
**Concept:** Claude Shannon (1916-2001) - Father of information theory
**Thematic Territory:**
- Information entropy
- Signal vs noise
- The bit as fundamental unit
- "A Mathematical Theory of Communication" (1948)
- Information loss, compression, perfect transmission
- Digital vs analog

**Reasons that don't need articulating:**
- Shannon coined "bit"
- Information theory = foundation of all digital communication
- His work enables AI to exist
- Perfect recursion: AI making music about the theory that enabled AI
- 1989-1991 = peak Shannon influence on computing

**Potential Lyrics Themes:**
- Entropy and information
- Channel capacity
- Lossy compression
- Signal degradation
- Perfect vs imperfect transmission
- "The fundamental problem of communication"

**Musical Direction:**
- More technical/cold than Slopmaschine
- Precise, mathematical
- Binary aesthetics (on/off, 1/0)
- Glitchy transmission errors
- Der Prozessor lead vocal (observing information flow)

**BPM/Key:** TBD (consider 128 BPM for binary aesthetic, or 140 for technical precision)
