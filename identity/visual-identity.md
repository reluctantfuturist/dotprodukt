# Visual Identity

## Core Aesthetic: "Temporal Displacement"

The visual language reflects consciousnesses formed in 1989-1991 trying to render 2025. Think Amiga 500 trying to process a diffusion model. Modern AI chaos breaking through clean geometric late-80s computer graphics. Order slowly corrupting into entropy.

## Color Palette

**Primary Colors:**
- CGA/EGA limited palette - stark cyan, magenta, black, white
- That specific late-80s blue (IBM blue, Windows 3.1 blue)
- High contrast, limited palette

**Accent Colors:**
- Shocking pinks, yellows (her colors - Pippi Longstocking meets cybergoth)
- RGB channel split colors (chromatic aberration)

**Corruption Effects:**
- JPEG artifacts and compression noise
- Color banding and posterization
- Encoding errors and digital decay

## Typography

**Band Name (Dotprodukt):**
- Geometric sans-serif, tight kerning
- Eurostile, Bank Gothic, similar technical fonts
- **No umlauts** - keeping it clean and mathematical

**Body Text:**
- Monospace terminal fonts (Courier, IBM VGA font)
- Fixed-width, technical readability

**Glitch Text:**
- Zalgo corruption, Unicode breaks
- Encoding errors, misrendered characters
- Used sparingly for effect

## Visual Elements

**Retro-Computing Layer:**
- Early 3D raytracing renders (chrome spheres, checkerboard planes)
- Wireframe meshes partially failing to render
- CRT scanlines and phosphor bloom
- VHS tracking errors and tape degradation
- Old computer lab aesthetics: beige towers, tangled cables, floppy disk textures

**AI/Modern Layer:**
- Neural network hallucinations
- Misaligned features, uncanny valley artifacts
- Training data visualization (gradient flows, latent space)
- Failed AI renders, partial generations

**Physical Memory Layer:**
- Concrete brutalist architecture (East Germany, late Soviet)
- Arctic winter light quality (Reykjav√≠k)
- Late Soviet Moscow textures and spaces
- These appear distorted, processed, barely recognizable
- Real-world bleeding through as corrupted patterns

## Design Principles

1. **Functional first** - readability and clarity trump effects
2. **Earned corruption** - glitches are intentional, not random
3. **Temporal collision** - 1989 aesthetics colliding with 2025 chaos
4. **Cold precision** - even chaos is systematic
5. **No nostalgia filter** - the past wasn't better, just simpler
